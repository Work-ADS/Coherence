Motion

Three durations. Three easings. Simple rules. Motion communicates hierarchy and confirms interaction — it's never decorative. Animate the container, not the text inside.

Duration

Duration scales with element size. A chip hover is 150ms; a modal entrance is 300ms. Never go below 100ms (invisible) or above 400ms (sluggish).

Rules

Do
  • Use transition: var(--duration-base) var(--easing-standard)
  • Animate transform and opacity (GPU-composited)
  • Provide @media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce) fallback
Don't
  • Never animate layout properties (width, height, top, left)
  • Never exceed 400ms — if it feels slow, the motion is wrong
  • Never animate text independently of its container
